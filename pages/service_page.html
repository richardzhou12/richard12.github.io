Service Process

1. Identifying Your Needs: Please check our product list to see if we already have what you are looking for. If not, we can also work with you to find a suitable product.

2. Finding the Product: We work with our partner factories to find a suitable product for you at a discounted price.

3. Quotation Report: We create a quotation report that includes the product details, price, and delivery time.

4. Reviewing the Quotation Report: We go over the quotation report with you and answer any questions you may have. If you are happy with the product and price, we proceed to the next step. If not, we will look for a better price or a more suitable product.

5. Payment: If you are satisfied with the product and price, we ask for a deposit of 30% of the total price via bank wire transfer to initiate the production process.

6. Production and Quality Check: After receiving the deposit, our partner factory will start producing the product. Once the product is finished, we will examine it to ensure that it meets your requirements.

7. Final Payment and Shipping: Once the product has passed the quality check, we ask you to pay the remaining 70% of the total price. After receiving payment, we will ship the product to you.

8. Follow-up: After the product has been delivered, we will follow up with you to ensure that you are satisfied with the product and service.


If you have any question for the above information, please contact us

Thank you for choosing our service. We are always ready to assist you in finding the right product at the right price.
